Private by architecture

Your data never becomes our business.

There is no ALOK OS server holding your life. The app runs on your device and syncs to a hidden folder in your own Google Drive — a place only you can reach.

Yours, on your Drive

Everything lives on your device and in your own Drive's app folder. Sign in on any device and it's all there.

Sealed vault

Passwords are encrypted with AES-256 using a key derived from your PIN. Without the PIN, the stored data is unreadable — anywhere.

Nothing collected

No ads, no analytics, no trackers. The optional reminder service receives only a time and a short title — never your content. Read the policy →
